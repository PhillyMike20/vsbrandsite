About me

Corporate training is my passion, and I have more than 15 years of instructional expertise in the corporate environment and workforce development. My clients have included Comcast, IBM, and AmerisourceBergen. I hold a Master of Arts degree in Business Administration/Management with a Psychology minor, and a Certificate in Training and Development. My complete resume can be viewed here.

I love watching people grow and will go the extra mile to make sure that each employee understands what's being taught.
