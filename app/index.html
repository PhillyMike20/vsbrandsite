About me

Training is my passion, and I have more than nine years of instructional expertise in the corporate environment. My clients have included Comcast, IBM, and AmerisourceBergen. I hold a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology with a Business Management minor and a Master of Arts degree in Business Administration/Management. My complete resume can be viewed here.

I love watching people grow and will go the extra mile to make sure that each person understands what's being taught.
